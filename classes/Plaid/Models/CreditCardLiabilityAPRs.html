Class Plaid::Models::CreditCardLiabilityAPRs < Plaid::Models::BaseModel

  • lib/plaid/models.rb

A representation of a credit card liability APR

Attributes
[R] apr_percentage

Annual Percentage Rate applied.
[R] apr_type

Enumerated response from the following options: “balance_transfer_apr”, “cash_apr”, “purchase_apr”, or “special”.
[R] balance_subject_to_apr

Amount of money that is subjected to the APR if a balance was carried beyond payment due date. How it is calculated can vary by card issuer. It is often calculated as an average daily balance.
[R] interest_charge_amount

Amount of money charged due to interest from last statement.
